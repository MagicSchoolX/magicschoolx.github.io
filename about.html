About Magic School X

Welcome to the magical world of roleplay and adventure. Learn about our server's history and the team that makes the magic happen.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Magic School X has grown into a thriving community of wizards and witches from around the world. Our server provides an immersive Hogwarts experience in Garry's Mod.

Our Mission

To create the most authentic and engaging magical roleplay experience, where every player can live out their wizarding world dreams and forge lasting friendships.

Community Values

We believe in fostering a welcoming, inclusive, and magical environment where creativity and storytelling flourish. Every wizard and witch has a place at Magic School X.
